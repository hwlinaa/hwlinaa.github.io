About Us
[span_0](start_span)

The Hong Kong New Generation Cultural Association is an education and training institution and a public charitable organization recognized by the government, established in 1974[span_0](end_span).

[span_1](start_span)

To firmly promote STEAM education, the Association established the Hong Kong New Generation Cultural Association Science Innovation Centre (SIC) in 2006[span_1](end_span).

[span_2](start_span)

To promote innovation and entrepreneurship to youth and facilitate relevant development in Hong Kong, HKNGCA established the Innovation and Entrepreneurship Center (IEC) in 2016[span_2](end_span).

[span_3](start_span)

We actively participate in the world's most prestigious international science competitions, including the Geneva International Exhibition of Inventions (Geneva) and the Regeneron International Science and Engineering Fair (ISEF)[span_3](end_span).
